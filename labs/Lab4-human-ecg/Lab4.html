Before Lab

Prepare for lab by:
  • Wear comfortable shoes to lab (you will be running up and down the stairs).
  • Watch cardiac cycle podcast.
  • Read human ecg protocol [Protocol 3].
  • Read the lab manual for this week ªs experiment [Lab 4] exploring the cardiac cycle and effects of exercise using ECG.
  • Write the [Prelab] in your lab notebook.
  • Do Quiz 4 on Laulima (open 24 hrs before lab).
